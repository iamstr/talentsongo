Who we are?

Talents on Go is a community based organization established in 2018 to be a source of inspiration to the youth in Kenya. The premise on which the organization is founded was to seek and contribute towards activities which aim to achieve its strategic objectives of helping the most affected in the community.

We believe by developing, mentoring and empowering the communities through teaching and facilitating entrepreneurial and life skills, mentorship in creativity, innovation, giving role models and support as a source of sustainable livelihood. We also hope to create employment by harnessing the potential that exists in the youths channeling them to productive activities, new opportunities and a better diverse community.

Talents on Go is upbeat about change and that change begins now
